# Words 2014/02/02

###Sturdy
adj. 1 结实的，坚固的
> * a sturdy table

2 (人或动物或身体)强壮的,健壮的
> * a man of sturdy build
> * sturdy legs

###Crooked [pronunciation]
adj. 1 不直的,弯曲的,扭曲的
> * a crooked nose

2 不诚实的,欺诈的
> * a crooked businessman/deal 奸商/不正当的交易

###Pliant
adj. 1 (人或人体) 柔顺的,绵软的
> * She lay pliant in his arm.

2 温顺的,容易摆布的
> * He was deposed and replaced by a more pliant successor.
